Frequent Asked Questions

How long does the service take?

The duration of the service will depend on the detail you've selected and prior condition of the vehicle.

The 301 Foam Wash and Detail approximate time is 2 hours and 30 minutes. The Metropolitan Waterless Detail approximate time is 3 hours and 30 minutes.

Please note that any additional services will require more time.
What is a waterless wash and it's benefits?

A waterless car wash is an eco-friendly and efficient car wash that uses little or no water at all.

Waterless washes are super-effective against painted surfaces that are sometimes difficult to clean without high-pressure water. Apart from this, a waterless car wash can potentially remove dirt, grease, and tar marks; without causing a single dent on the vehicle!

You can prepare for your detail appointment by gathering all loose and personal items. Please ensuring that the car is accessible by unlocking the doors. You can also add specific instructions for the detailer to follow on arrival within the booking notes.
