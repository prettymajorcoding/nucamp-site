About Us

Wash & Wax D.M.V. understands that you may have a busy schedule, with many things to accomplish; that is why we come to you. Whether you are at work on your lunch break or at the mall running errands or at home preparing your dinner, we will service your vehicle where ever, whenever!

The Waterless Process

Spray

Spray a light mist of biodegradable, organic, and water-less car wash solution to the surface of the vehicle to soften, lift, and dissolve dirt.

Wipe

Gently wipe with a microfiber towel and lift to pick up grime from vehicle's surface.

Buff

Using a second microfiber towel buff to receive a mirror like flawless finish that last for weeks.
